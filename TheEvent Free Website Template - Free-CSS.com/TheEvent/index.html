About The Event

Dear Teacher,

If We Had Not Found You As Our Teacher Years Ago, Our Life Must Be Incredibly Different You Opened Our Eyes, What We Could Do, In A Way That We've Never Thought Possible. You Took Accepted Us As Kids And Made Us See The World And Beyond. Therefore, It Is Incredibly Important For Us To Give Back To Them What They Rightfully Deserve.

Where

VISMAYA PARTY HALL
Address: 1268, Magadi Main Rd, Govindaraja Nagar Ward, Govindaraja Nagar, Vijayanagar, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560040, India



When

Sunday
5th March 2023

Things to Remember

Here is a list important things you need to keep in your mind.

Registrations

Every student who wish to attend the reunion Must be registered in advance. Hence, please feel free to get your name registered if it's not done yet.

Timings

We request all the students to be present at the venue by 4:30 PM to start the event on time.

Seating

We have reserved few front row seats for your beloved teachers, so please feel free to ask volunteers if you have any questions

Volunteers

We have plenty of volunteers who can help you with your queries, please reach out to them and get your questions answered anytime during or before the event

Participation

We request everyone to participate in this event as it is you who can make this even a success. We'd want you to be active in the events.

Safety

Your safety is our priority, we respect others personal space and we'd trust that you'd do the same. You can reach out to us if you need any assistance.
